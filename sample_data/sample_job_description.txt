Job Title: Backend Engineer

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement backend services and APIs.
- Work with databases to store and retrieve application data.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define system requirements.
- Ensure system reliability and maintainability.

Requirements:
- 3+ years of backend development experience.
- Proficiency in Python or similar backend language.
- Experience designing RESTful APIs.
- Understanding of basic system design principles.
- Familiarity with cloud environments is a plus.

Nice to Have:
- Exposure to AI-assisted systems.
- Experience handling background jobs or asynchronous workflows.
